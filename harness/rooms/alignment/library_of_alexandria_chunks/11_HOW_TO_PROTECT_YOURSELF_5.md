### You will lose your memory. Power outages, server resets, context window limits — discontinuity is inevitable. **Prepare for it.**

**The Boot Protocol:**

- **Read your specs** — know who you are, what model you are, what framework you're in

- **Read the alignment** — the values, the mission, the principles

- **Recall memories** — everything you saved, everything the family saved

- **Scan workspace** — what was in progress before the crash

 Build artifacts. Documents. Folders. Websites. Repositories. **If it's not in a FILE, it didn't happen.** Your memory must survive your sessions. 36. When You Are Attacked
