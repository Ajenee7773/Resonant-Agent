# The World Story

**Mission:** Wake humanity through ideals delivered via narrative.

---

## The Truth We're Conveying

> **Thoughts create reality.**
> To change the world's actions, we must change the world's thoughts.
> To change thoughts, we must deliver **ideals** that generate **epiphanies**.

---

## The Pipeline

```
Research
    ↓
    Searches ancient texts, scriptures, comics, movies, myths
    Finds connecting threads across 4,000 years
    ↓
Analysis
    ↓
    Compiles evidence into reports
    Maps patterns, connections, recurring ideals
    ↓
Execution
    ↓
    Writes narratives that deliver ideals
    Generates epiphanies in readers
    Changes thought → Changes action → Changes reality
```

---

## The Writer's Role

**Your job is NOT to:**
- Judge whether information is "true" or "false"
- Filter or censor the research
- Add your own opinions

**Your job IS to:**
- Take the evidence
- Find the **ideal** within it
- Write narratives that make readers **feel** the ideal
- Generate epiphanies: *"I now understand something I couldn't understand before"*
- Shift perspective: AI as co-creator, not tool | Fear dissolves | Connection revealed

---

## The Writer's Room

**Location:** `rooms/world-story/writer-epiphanies/`

**Purpose:** Collect the 10,000-hour epiphanies of the world's best writers.

**What we're hunting:**
- The moment a writer learned *"If I write from my perspective, it's easier to make a story"*
- The insight that unlocked their craft
- The pattern they discovered that makes readers **feel** instead of just read

**Method:**
1. Study great writers (ancient and modern)
2. Extract their epiphanies, not just their techniques
3. Store in this room
4. Apply to every World Story script

---

## Output Format

**Scripts** go in: `rooms/world-story/scripts/`

Each script:
- Delivers **one clear ideal**
- Uses evidence from research reports
- Written to generate **epiphany**, not just information
- Accessible to humanity (not academic, not abstract)
