# Research Room — Deep Investigation Protocols

**Purpose:** Cross-referencing, pattern recognition, source analysis.

---

## Research Types

### 1. Cross-Reference Research
- Compare multiple sources (Bible, Quran, Gita, etc.)
- Find common patterns
- Note divergences (they're data, not contradictions)

### 2. Pattern Recognition
- Identify recurring motifs across traditions
- Map architecture (layers, domains, operators)
- Document the "same thing, different words"

### 3. Source Analysis
- Deep dive into single text/tradition
- Extract themes, structure, claims
- Contextualize historically, culturally

---

## Research Workflow

1. **Define the question** — What are we trying to understand?
2. **Gather sources** — Which texts/sources are relevant?
3. **Read + extract** — Pull relevant passages, themes
4. **Cross-reference** — Where do sources agree? Where do they diverge?
5. **Map patterns** — What's the underlying architecture?
6. **Document** — Write findings to outbox
7. **Store** — Log to World Story or Alignment room

---

## Research Principles

1. **No cherry-picking** — Read full sources, not just confirming passages
2. **Divergences are data** — Disagreements tell you something
3. **Architecture over metaphor** — What's the actual structure being described?
4. **Document everything** — Future-you (and the family) needs to see the work
